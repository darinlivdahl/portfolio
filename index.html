Experience

Web Development

My journey into web development began way back in 1997 with Adobe PageMill 2.0 consisting mostly of frames and image maps. It wasn’t until I began looking “under the hood” at code that I acquired a deep interest. Since then I’ve logged countless hours devoted to custom coding, utilizing CSS frameworks and JavaScript libraries. My new passion is Node.js and React.

Web Design

For much of my career I’ve been fortunate to work with many skilled web designers to help build their creations for the web. On other occasions I have provided my own designs for both large and small websites on platforms such as WordPress and Salesforce. Lately, I utilize tools such as Adobe XD and Figma for mockups, interactive wireframes and prototyping components.

Technical Consulting

The latter 5+ years of my career I’ve spent consulting clients who use the Salesforce platform. This has entailed creating enterprise email marketing templates in Marketing Cloud and setting up partner community sites in Experience Cloud. Other activities include technical documentation, story refinement, Apex and Lightning Web Component development.

More work experience details can be found on my LinkedIn profile.
